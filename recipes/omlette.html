Omellete

Description

A true French omelette, or omelet as Americans call it, is just eggs and butter, no filling. The egg is folded for a soft, tender texture. It's 10% ingredients and 90% technique, so it does take a bit of practice to perfect.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon cold water
  • 1/4 kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • white pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs and salt in a mixing bowl
  2. Heat the butter on a nonstick pan
  3. Pour the eggs in and shake the pan to level the mixture
  4. Roll the omelette into a cylinder
  5. Flip onto a plate
