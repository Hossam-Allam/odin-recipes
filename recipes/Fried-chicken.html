Fried Chicken

Description

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Make the breading by combining the flour and seasonings
  2. Dredge the chicken in Buttermilk
  3. Place the chicken in the flour mixture
  4. Fry until golden
